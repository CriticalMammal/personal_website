Project Micro

Source Code

Windows .exe Download coming soon!

More of an experiment with perspective and tile maps than a game. You can zoom in and out to pretty extreme amounts, and inside each "microchip" is another microchip you can take control of and move around. Collision detection is implemented, the hot pink tiles can't be passed through.

There is a limitation to the zoom amount however, due to the way I implemented it. It doesn't go on forever, after you enter roughly 15 microchips the maximum floating point value is reached, and the visuals spaz out. It's pretty interesting, and I didn't end up changing that limitation because I enjoyed the idea of having that barrier for the smallest thing that could exist.
